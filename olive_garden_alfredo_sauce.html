Olive Garden Alfredo Sauce

Description

Supposed original Olive Garden Alfredo Sauce recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 oz butter
  • 1 tablespoon garlic
  • 2 tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup Romano cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    •

    Steps

    1. Combine butter and garlic in sauce pan. Sautee over medium heat for one minute
    2. Add all purpose flour
    3. Add milk and heavy cream
    4. Add Parmesan and Romano cheese
